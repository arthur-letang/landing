In a few words

Stade de Reims is a French football club, founded on June 18, 1931 in Reims, in the department of Marne. The club has been chaired by Jean-Pierre Caillot since 2004 and managed by Óscar García Junyent since July 2021. The first team plays in the Ligue 1 Championship.
